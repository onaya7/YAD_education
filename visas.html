Student Visa Advisory Service

Every country has its specific requirements and procedures for visa applications and the application rules may differ from time to time. This can create quite a challenge for students preparing to go abroad. The validity period for visas also varies depending on the type of studies be it undergraduate or postgraduate studies at universities.

YAD Education’s team of professional advisors keeps up to date on the latest announcements regarding international student visas in other countries. Whether you are applying for an undergraduate or postgraduate programme to the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Ireland, or another country. Once your placement for the university's programme has been confirmed, our advisor will assist you in preparing the relevant documents to apply for your student visa.

Pro tip: There is really no standard processing time for visa applications. So, we suggest you get your documents ready as early as possible to ensure a smooth departure.
